My Story

I would not be where I am today without my long work history in the service industry and retail. Prior to entering the corporate workforce, I worked as a bartender which fulfilled my love for customer service and allowed me to meet people from all walks of life. I was first exposed to marketing when I took on the position of social media manager for Houndsooth Sports Bar, allowing me to explore a new interest and utilize the visual design and marketing skills that I learned in my academic program.

Outside of the professional world, I enjoy spending time with my dog, Stitch, as well as dancing, skiing, and traveling. I love learning about other cultures and one day hope to experience as many as I can firsthand.
